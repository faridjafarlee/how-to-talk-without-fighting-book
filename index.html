Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the book and the approach.

Will this work if my partner doesn't read it?
Absolutely. The book focuses on changing your side of the dynamic. When one person changes the way they speak and listen (using tools like de-escalation and the pause), the entire conversation shifts. You can't control them, but you can influence the dynamic.
Is this for couples only?
No. While there is a dedicated chapter for partners, the core principles (nervous system regulation, listening without rehearsing, boundaries) apply to everyone. There are specific chapters for Parents, Workplace, and Friendships as well.
How is this different from other communication books?
Many books focus on "scripts." This book focuses on biology. It teaches you how to calm your nervous system so you can actually use the scripts when you're angry. It bridges the gap between knowing what to say and actually being able to say it.
